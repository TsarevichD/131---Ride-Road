Ride Road

Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to Ride Road, the ultimate vehicle collection app! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

By using Ride Road, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Profile Information: Name, age, and profile photo (optional)
  • Vehicle Data: Car and motorcycle collection details, including names, brands, years, and estimated values
  • Collection Data: Vehicle collections, locations, and preferences

Usage Information

  • App Usage: How you interact with the app, features used, and time spent
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and app version
  • Performance Data: Crash reports and app performance metrics

How We Use Your Information

  • App Functionality: To provide and improve our vehicle collection services
  • Personalization: To customize your experience and recommendations
  • Analytics: To understand app usage and improve performance
  • Support: To provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • Achievements: To track and display your collection milestones

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored locally on your device using secure iOS storage mechanisms. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Local Storage: All data is stored on your device using iOS Keychain and UserDefaults
  • Encryption: Sensitive data is encrypted using iOS security frameworks
  • No Cloud Sync: Your data remains on your device and is not transmitted to external servers
  • Privacy First: We don't collect or share your personal information with third parties

Third-Party Services

Ride Road may use the following third-party services:

  • Analytics: Anonymous usage analytics to improve app performance
  • Crash Reporting: Crash reports to identify and fix issues
  • App Store: Standard iOS App Store analytics

These services do not have access to your personal vehicle collection data.

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: View all data stored in the app
  • Modify: Edit or update your profile and collection information
  • Delete: Remove individual vehicles or entire collections
  • Export: Export your collection data (if supported)
  • Reset: Clear all app data and start fresh

Children's Privacy

Ride Road is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending a notification through the app (if applicable)

Your continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the new Privacy Policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@rideroad.app
  • App Support: Through the in-app support feature
  • Website: www.rideroad.app
